# 🎯 ZINGLE - Logo & Brand Identity

## Logo Overview

**Zingle** logo is a modern, dynamic geometric symbol representing connection, attraction, and the intersection of two people. The design features interlocking curved ribbons forming an abstract 'Z' connection figure with a vibrant gradient.

---

## Logo Design Specification

### Visual Description

```
A high-resolution vector-style logo centered on a clean white background. 
The logo is a precise, interlocking geometric connection symbol, created 
by interwoven curved ribbons. Two mirrored 'S' curves connect to form a 
complex, dynamic central knot, making an abstract 'Z' connection figure. 
The ribbons feature a vibrant, seamless hot pink-to-bright orange gradient 
flow that transitions across the entire interlocking form. All edges are 
perfectly sharp and smooth.
```

### Design Elements

#### **Geometry**
- **Shape**: Abstract 'Z' formed by interlocking curved ribbons
- **Curves**: Two mirrored 'S' curves creating a dynamic connection knot
- **Style**: Precise, geometric, contemporary vector design
- **Edges**: Sharp and smooth with perfect curves

#### **Color Palette**

**Primary Gradient**: Hot Pink → Bright Orange

```
Entry Point:   #FF5864 (Hot Pink / Tinder Red)
                    ↓ (smooth gradient transition)
End Point:     #FF8A00 (Bright Orange)
```

**Alternative Colors**:
- **Monochrome**: #000000 (for print/simplicity)
- **Inverse**: #FFFFFF (on dark backgrounds)

#### **Dimensions**
- **Aspect Ratio**: 1:1 (Square)
- **Minimum Size**: 32x32px (digital)
- **Recommended Size**: 256x256px (standard)
- **Print Size**: 1 inch (at 300 DPI minimum)

## Logo Usage Guidelines

### ✅ DO

- ✅ Use the primary gradient logo on white backgrounds
- ✅ Use monochrome on documents and print
- ✅ Use inverse on dark backgrounds
- ✅ Maintain clear space around logo (1/4 of logo width)
- ✅ Keep edges sharp and smooth
- ✅ Scale proportionally
- ✅ Use on brand-approved surfaces

### ❌ DON'T

- ❌ Distort or stretch the logo
- ❌ Rotate the logo (except 90° for specific layouts)
- ❌ Add effects (drop shadows, glows, etc.)
- ❌ Change colors without approval
- ❌ Use on conflicting backgrounds without contrast check
- ❌ Place logo too close to other elements
- ❌ Simplify or modify the geometry
